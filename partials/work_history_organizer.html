Machine Learning Meetup

October 2012-February 2023

Organizer

The Machine Learning Meetup is a meetup group in the Vancouver area with a focus on machine learning and related software engineering, computer science and data science topics.

I served as organizer for a decade.

Together with the Data Science Meetup and the Learn Data Science Meetup, I helped turned all three, including the Machine Learning Meetup, into the the largest communities for artificial intelligence (AI), data science, and machine learning in western Canada.

My organizing activities include, organizing and hosting meetings and presentations, arranging speakers for meetings, helping (some) speakers develop presentations for meetings, and networking.
