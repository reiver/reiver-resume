Machine Learning Meetup

October 2012-February 2023

Organizer

The Machine Learning Meetup is a meetup group in the Vancouver area with a focus on machine learning and related software engineering, computer science and data science topics.

I served as organizer for a decade.

Together with the Data Science Meetup and the Learn Data Science Meetup, I helped turned all three, including the Machine Learning Meetup, into the the largest communities for artificial intelligence (AI), data science, and machine learning in western Canada.

As the organizer for the Machine Learning Meetup —

  • I organized meet-ups,
  • I recruited people to create and give presentations,
  • I helped (some) speakers develop their presentations for the meet-up,
  • I formed relationships with organizations that could provide space for us to hold meet-ups,
  • I scheduled meet-up events,
  • I promoted meet-up events,
  • I prepared the space that was used to host the meet-up event,
  • I was the master-of-ceremonies for the meet-up events,
  • I orchestrated the meet-up events,
  • I networked with people who attened the meet-ups,
  • I created (online and in-person) spaces for the community to form,
  • I engaged in conversation with the community,
  • I moderated online community spaces,
  • I recruited co-organizers.
