#SpaceHost

November 2022 - Present

Senior Principal Software Engineer & Architect

#SpaceHost provides fully-managed hosting for the Fediverse — a decentralized social-media network. Including hosting popular Fediverse software, such as: Akkoma, Castopod, Firefish, Friendica, Gitea, Kbin, Lemmy, Mastodon, Owncast, PeerTube, Pixelfed, Pleroma, and more.

As as a senior principal software engineer at #SpaceHost —

  • Architectural Leadership — I led the design and implementation the #SpaceHost systems architecture.
  • Technical Vision — I provided the long-term technical vision for products, software systems, technologies and infrastructure. Often this amounted to looking at our long term needs and deciding what should be done.
  • Roadmap Development — I defined and communicated the technical vision for #SpaceHost, and aligned the organization towards those goals.
  • Technical Oversight & Guidance — I provided technical oversight and guidance to (some of the) other software engineers and system architects, both in formal and informal settings. Reviewing (some amount of the) major designs and architecture decisions from engineers' & architects' and providing feedback when needed; as well as helping to overcome architecture problems that may have been found in the review process.
  • Technical Due Diligence — I performed detailed analysis of external decentralized social-media networking software and protocols.
  • Mentoring and Coaching — I acted as a role model and mentored other developers, advocating for technical principles and helping to grow the team's and organization's capacity and expertise.
  • Training — I dealt with training. This included training (some of the) newly hired software engineer (at various levels). This also included training (some of the) software engineers (at various levels) already on staff, when we needed them to become competent at a new skill.
  • On-Boarding — I dealt with on-boarding. This included creating and refining training materials for software engineers (at various levels). This also included identifying and filling in any gaps in the knowledge of the newly hired software engineer.
  • Hiring — I dealt with hiring. This included interviewing and screening software engineering an architect candidates. This also included training other people to learn how to hire, interview, and screen.
  • Process Improvement — I designed, developed, and enhanced processes with a focus on security, reliability, and performance.
  • External Representation — I acted as a spokesperson for #SpaceHost at industry events, at conferences, on podcasts, and on social-media.
  • Development — I designed and build (some of the) applications and infrastructure related to the Fediverse and its protocol — acct-URI, ActivityPub, ActivityStreams, host-meta, mastodon-API (client-server), WebFinger, etc.
  • The work was done using the Go (i.e., Golang) programming language and Postgres database.
