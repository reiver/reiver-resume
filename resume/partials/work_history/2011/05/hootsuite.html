HootSuite

May 2011 - September 2012

Engineering Lead & Senior Software Engineer

Hootsuite is a popular social-media management platform. And was at one time the №1 Twitter application in the world. Today Hootsute is a has more than 1,000 people on staff, has 13 office spread out across the world, and is worth around $1 billions, but —

I joined Hootsuite whlie it was still a startup. When there were a handful of us working in a back-room behind the Invoke office.

I created large parts of the early Hootsuite application — both backend, and frontend. I was the engineering lead for 4 teams, while also being a senior software engineer writing and shipping code.

  • I created Hootsute's Facebook integration from scratch,
  • I created Hootsute's Foursquare integration from scratch,
  • I created Hootsute's Instagram integration from scratch,
  • I created Hootsuite's LinkedIn integration from scratch, and
  • I created Hootsuite app directory from scratch.

Also —

  • I worked on Hootsuite's Twitter integration, and
  • I worked on Hootsuite's Google+ integration.

I also later took over:

  • Hootsuite's analytics system, and
  • Hootsuite's webfeed sytem.

At HootSuite I operated as the Engineering Lead for the Analytics Team, and had senior roles on the App Directory Team, the Acquisitions Integrations Team, and the Web Team.

As an engineering lead at Hootsuite my day-to-day responsibilities included —

  • software engineering,
  • system architecture,
  • research and development,
  • technical oversight,
  • onboarding new engineers,
  • mentoring of (more) junior engineers,
  • performing design reviews of other engineers' designs,
  • performing code reviews on other engineers' code,
  • knowledge transfer to new engineers, and
  • ensuring, encouraging and enforcing the quality of engineering work of other engineers working on the Analytics Team, Acquisitions Integrations Team, and certain parts of the Web Team.

The work was done using the PHP, Scala, and JavaScript programming languages, the MySQL and MongoDB database servers, and the Nginx web server.
