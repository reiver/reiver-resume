RobinFeed

August 2024 - Present

Senior Principal Software Engineer & Architect

RobinFeed is a custom feed generator for the Bluesky social-media network.

I joined RobinFeed at its beginning. The team was small — just 3 of us. We got a lot done in a short period of time — and shipped the MVP in a little over 1 month.

  • Developed and shipped a scalable backend, search-engine, infrastructue and the large-scale data-processing infrastructure, from scratch for the Bluesky social-media network ecosystem.
  • Rapidly learned the Bluesky suite of social-media networking protocols — AT-protocol (i.e., ATProto), AT-URI, bsky-protocol, CAR (content addressible archive), CBOR (concise binary object representation), DID (decentralized identifiers), DID-PLC (DID placeholder), NSID (namespaced identifiers), XRPC — and implemented each Bluesky protocol from scratch.
  • The work was done using the Go (i.e., Golang) programming language.
