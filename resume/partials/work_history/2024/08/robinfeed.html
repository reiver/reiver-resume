RobinFeed

August 2024 - Present

Go Developer

RobinFeed is a custom feed generator for the Bluesky social-media network.

I joined RobinFeed at its beginning. The team was small — just 3 of us. We got a lot done in a short period of time — and shipped the MVP in a little over 1 month.

  • I developed and shipped:
    • a scalable backend,
    • a search-engine,
    • infrastructue, and
    • large-scale data-processing infrastructure
    ... from scratch for the Bluesky social-media network ecosystem.
  • I rapidly learned the Bluesky suite of social-media networking protocols —
    • AT-protocol (i.e., ATProto),
    • AT-URI,
    • bsky-protocol,
    • CAR (content addressible archive),
    • CBOR (concise binary object representation),
    • DID (decentralized identifiers),
    • DID-PLC (DID placeholder),
    • NSID (namespaced identifiers),
    • XRPC
    — and implemented each Bluesky protocol from scratch.

The work was done using the Go (i.e., Golang) programming language, and the SQLite & BigQuery database systems.
